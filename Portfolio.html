A Little About Me

Sam Thach

I am currently a fifth year at the Oregon Institute of Technology with a major in data science and a minor in geographical information systems. My expected graduation date is in June 2024 and I plan on pursuing a Master's in Data Science and then a Ph.D in Data Science. I am originally from Orange County California and grew up in "Little Saigon", which is the largest Vietnamese community in America, but decided to go to Oregon Tech to experience new things I could not have at a nearby university.

I am working as a student tech ambassador, a supplemental instructor, and the student safety patrol lead at Oregon Tech and previously worked as a research assistant. I was a research assistant in 2021 and presented my results at the 2022 Special Interest Group Computer Science student competition. In March of 2022, I joined on as a student tech ambassador and assist the Oregon Tech admissions team by providing tours, helping with events, and doing general office work. The same year, I also began working as a supplemental instructor for the Oregon Tech Math Department and assist professors with grading, tutoring, and answering students' questions in class. One project I am currently working on is for FlowJo LLC to create a machine learning powered module for their software application.
