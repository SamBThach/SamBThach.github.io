More About Me

Sam Thach

I am originally from Orange County California and grew up in "Little Saigon" which is the largest Vietnamese community in America. I am currently a third year at the Oregon Institute of Technology with a major in data science and a minor in geographical information systems. My expected graduation date is in June 2023 and I plan on pursuing a Master's in Data Science or a PH.D in Data Science. I decided on going to an out of state college because I believed it would allow me to experience new things I otherwise would not be able to if I was in state.

I am working part-time as student patrol lead with campus safety, as a research assistant at Oregon Tech, and as the data science student ambassador. I am a member of the Wellness Committee at Oregon Tech and have sent out surveys to students and staff that will hopefully guide the Wellness Committee's future actions.
